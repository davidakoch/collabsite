Developers Against Animal Testing

Animal testing, or vivisction, is a creul and out-dated means of obtaining information about the affects of drugs and cosmetics. Developers Against Animal Testing (DAAT) is comprised of a small group of junior web developers who are dedicated to ending animal testing. We are taking a stand against the abuse of animals by engaging with a network of caring individuals and organizations.


To learn more about the history of animal testing, click here.

More anti-vivisection resources and organizations:
  • Leaping Bunny offers information about which companies test on animals.
  • The Animal Liberation Front is an activist organization with a world-wide network.
  • Physicians Committee for Responsible Medicine is a DC-based organization that uses eduation and legislation to put an end to vivisection.
  • LUSH Cosmetics is an vehemently anti-animal testing cosmetics line with many locations across the world.
